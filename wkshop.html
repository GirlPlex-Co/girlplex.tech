College Application Panel

Despite an ongoing pandemic, students across the world are still faced with college applications and worries about school. In an effort to help, GirlPlex will be hosting its first panel for high school students to get help from college students enrolled in Princeton University, Cornell University, Harvey Mudd, UCLA, and multiple others. You can come prepared with questions or tune in to listen to the panelists talk about what made them stand out on their application, the inspiration for their essays, how they decided this college was perfect for them and much more!
