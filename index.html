About

GirlPlex. Closing the gender gap.

GirlPlex is focused on closing the gender gap in computer science. Currently, women are very underrepresented in computer science fields. GirlPlex aims to educated girls in computer science from middle school, so they will be more likely to pursue computer science fields in high school.

Winter Classes

We are currently hosting winter classes as a part of a fundraiser for Cradles to Crayons. Classes are offered for Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All age groups are welcome (3rd through 12th grade). Although students are not required to donate, we encourage all students to donate $5 to the fundraiser. Donate to the fundraiser and sign up for classes today!

Middle School Summer Camp

The middle school summer camp focuses on teaching middle school girls the basics of web development. This camp strives to prepare middle school girls for future computer science classes in high school, and give them more confidence to participate in a mostly male-dominated classroom. We are proud to announce that we have held two successful summers of the middle school summer camp!

Elementary School Summer Camp

The elementary school summer camp is GirlPlex's newest event. This summer camp is open to both girls and boys, and aims to introduce children to a mixed-gender classroom from a young age, which will hopefully encourage more women to pursue computer science from a young age and create a better atmosphere for women in technology.
