GirlPlex. Closing the gender gap in computer science.

GirlPlex focuses on closing the gender gap in computer science. Currently, women are very underrepresented in STEM related fields, especially computer science. GirlPlex aims to introduce more girls to coding and boost their confidence, encouraging them to pursue a career in technology and close the gender gap.

GirlPlex strives to provide a variety of programs such as:

  • Middle School Camps for girls interested in computer science
  • Elementary School Camps for children to learn how to code in a co-ed enviornment
  • Different workshops and panels for girls interested in STEM, such as a College Application Panel
