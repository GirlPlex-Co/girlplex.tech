Elementary School Summer Camp

Looking to learn in an inclusive place where your ideas will be heard? Interested in changing the computer science mindset towards females in tech? Join our elementary school summer camp! Our virtual elementary school summer camp is open to both boys and girls. GirlPlex aims to introduce students to a mixed-gender computer science enviornment from a young age to increase young females' confidence in their abilities. We are proud to announce that we have held our first succesful elementary school camp with over 90 scholars! The scholars worked in co-ed teams to create their amazing final projects!
